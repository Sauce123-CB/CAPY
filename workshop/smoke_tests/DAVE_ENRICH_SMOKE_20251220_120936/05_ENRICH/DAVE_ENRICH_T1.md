## Narrative I: Investment Thesis Synthesis (State 2)

### Business Model

Dave Inc. is a U.S. neobank targeting financially underserved consumers—the ~185 million Americans classified as "financially coping or vulnerable" who live paycheck-to-paycheck. The company operates a mobile-first platform with two core revenue drivers:

**1. ExtraCash (Primary Revenue Driver, ~92% of Service-Based Revenue)**
- Short-term overdraft product offering $25-$500 advances with ~11-day average duration
- Revenue model: 5% mandatory overdraft service fee (capped at $5 minimum, $15 maximum) plus optional instant transfer fees (simplified structure implemented December 2024, eliminating controversial "tips" model)
- Underwritten by proprietary CashAI engine using real-time bank transaction data (no FICO)
- Q3 2025: $2.0B quarterly originations, 4.8% net monetization rate, $207 average advance size

**2. Dave Card / Checking (Secondary Driver, ~8% of Revenue)**
- FDIC-insured demand deposit accounts with debit card issued through bank partners (transitioned from Evolve Bank to Coastal Community Bank in Q2 2025)
- Revenue from interchange (Durbin-exempt), ATM fees, deposit referral fees, instant withdrawal fees
- Q3 2025: $510M quarterly card spend, 25% YoY growth

**Unit Economics (Q3 2025):**
- CAC: $19 per new member (trending toward $22 as easy acquisition exhausts)
- ARPU: $217 annualized (39% YoY growth)
- LTV/CAC: 8:1 (industry-leading)
- Gross Profit Margin: 69% (non-GAAP)
- Adj. EBITDA Margin: 39%

3. **Network Effects (Nascent but Growing):** 40% of new users come from referrals (RQ6), suggesting viral product-market fit in target demographic. Growing member base (13.5M total, 2.77M MTMs) improves AI model quality.
4. **Regulatory Arbitrage:** Durbin-exempt interchange (~4x higher than large banks) as sub-$10B asset issuer via bank partners. However, RQ evidence identifies this as temporary/at-risk (see Weaknesses).
