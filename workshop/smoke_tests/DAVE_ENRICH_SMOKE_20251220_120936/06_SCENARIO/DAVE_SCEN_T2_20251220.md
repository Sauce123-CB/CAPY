## Limitations and Caveats

1. **S4 Modeling:** Acquisition scenario (S4) was modeled with $0 impact due to empty GIM overlay in T1. The intended acquisition premium (25-40%) would require post-SSE terminal value adjustment, not implemented in this kernel run.

2. **Scenario Independence:** The SSE assumes additive impacts for co-occurring scenarios. Non-linear interactions (e.g., S1 fee restrictions affecting S2 BNPL pricing) are not explicitly modeled.

3. **Constraint-Light Structure:** No causal dependencies, MECE groups, or economic incompatibilities were applied, yielding 16 feasible states. In practice, some states (e.g., S1 + S4) may have lower joint probabilities due to unmodeled dependencies.

4. **BNPL Revenue Trajectory:** S2 assumes 15-20% revenue contribution by Y5, but precedents range widely (Block 17% in 2 years vs. SoFi 41% in 7-8 years). Actual trajectory depends on merchant acquisition success.

5. **Regulatory Settlement Timing:** S1 assumes settlement within 12-18 months, but litigation could extend longer or escalate, shifting probabilities and magnitudes.
